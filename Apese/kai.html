MEET MRS. KAI IDOKO

KAI IDOKO

PROJECT CO ORDINATOR

Kai previously served as an Immediate Explosives Manager at Pyrotechnics Company Limited and volunteered with Akili Dada’s African Women Lead Programs, championing leadership development among young women. Her humanitarian experience includes working as a Food Distribution Enumerator with World Vision Kenya, a Data Clerk with Dalberg Institute for the German Corporation (GIZ), and contributing to the Ishtar MSM CLM Project funded by PEPFAR and the Global Fund. A Bachelor of Education degree holder, Kai is a dedicated Gender Activist and Human Rights Defender, passionate about advancing women’s rights, girls’ education, and equality. Her strength lies in her ability to engage collaboratively and communicate effectively across all levels of stakeholders. She currently serves as the Project Coordinator at Apese Ere, where she leads the implementation of the HIV/TB Program, working to improve health outcomes through community outreach and service delivery.
